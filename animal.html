ANIMAL TESTING FOR COSMETICS

What is "animal testing for COSMETICS?"

> animals have been used in painful tests to demonstrate that cosmetics are effective and safe to use
> transferring the results of animal tests to humans has proven to be problematic and misleading in many cases!!
> nevertheless, countless animals are still subjected to cruel tests around the world

What Animals are being tested on Cosmetics??

approximately 100,000-200,000 animals suffer and die just for cosmetics every year around the world
  • rabbits
  • guinea pigs
  • hamsters
  • rats and mice

What tests are carried out on Animals?

> skin and eye irritation tests : chemicals are rubbed onto skins or dripped into animals' eyes
> repeated oral force-feeding for weeks to look for general illness
> these tests often causes a large amount of pain and effects like blindness, organ damage or even death
> these animals are often experimented to death or killed by medicine

Are there alnernative choices for animal testing or solutions?

  • companies can test the chemicals on 3D cornea made out of human tissues instead of dripping them into rabbits' eyes
  • use established ingredients -> no need to test new substances
