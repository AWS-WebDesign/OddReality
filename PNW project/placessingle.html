STATIC ACTIVITY : PEEK FALL COLOR (leaves)

> Enjoy the leaves turning into fall color in Selkirk Loop in Idaho!

> The International Selkirk Loop is an acclaimed 280-mile scenic byway that passes through British Columbia in Canada and
Washington and Idaho in the United States

> However, the best leaf views are along the Idaho portion!
